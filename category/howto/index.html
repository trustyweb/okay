VPRD based on OS Linux Mint 17.1 and SW NoMachine

It’s the simplest way, clock 10 minutes.

in more detail
The first one is the simplest and fastest, it’s based on popular Open Source of Linux Mint, and free remote access SW from authoritative NX Nomachine. The protocol from Nomachine is the best solution for limitless multimedia at the moment – you can watch videos and play games on a remote computer without any troubles. Even from a tablet and smart phone.

Any VPS-hoster that allows to download an ISO image and work with a VPS through a control panel integrated VNC Console would suit us. For example, let’s take vultr.com – the hoster that meets all our requirements. It’s great that with Vultr you can choose the point of presence of your VPRD from a long list of locations form all around the world. The rental prices are low, and the payment is available even with bitcoin.

  • Enter http://vultr.com/ and check in. *pleasant bonus – after topping up, you’ll get additional 5$.

  • Top up in any convenient way – minimum 5$. *remember to confirm your account be reference sent on your email.

  • After topping up, upload the install iso image of OS Linux Mint. In this regard, on your account enter the section “Upload Iso” and insert the link for the distributive in the field “Remote URL:” The link is given later:
http://linuxfreedom.com/linuxmint/stable/17.1/linuxmint-17.1-mate-64bit.iso

When the iso image is uploaded completely, the status is changed to “Available”

  • Great. Go to the section “Deploy” and create our first private remote computer:
In more detail
• Location is a point of presence of a remote computer. Choose as you wish, the point is, you have to remember that the closer to you is the server the lower is ping, which means the VPRD speed is higher.

• Operating System – choose the property page 64 bit and the option Custom. Than in Virtual ISO designate the install iso image uploaded earlier linuxmint-17.1-mate-64bit.iso

• Server Size is the capacity of our VPRD. Such characteristics as the quantity of processor cores, DDR RAM and capacity of a SSD disk. It’s recommended to have from 2 CPU and 2gb of DDR RAM for VPRD based on a desktop-distributive to work in comfort.

• Server Label is the name of a private remote computer.

  • Recently created VPRD can be seen in the section My Servers. Wait till the status of the server change to “Running” then click “Manage”

  • That’s it, now you can start installing OS Linux Mint. In this regard click “View Console” – the window with the graphics screen of the operating system installation will open.

Installation:

*the language, time zone and keyboard layout are chosen as you wish.

  • Great, the most important things are done, there is a little to do. After the installation, click “Restart Now” (*see above) and close the window of the VNS Console. Now it’s necessary to remove the install iso image from the virtual computer:

  • Enter the VNS Console again clicking the button “View Console” in the subsection Server Actions. Wait for Linux to upload, choose the account and insert the corresponding password:

  • Now it’s necessary to download and install the server of the remote access of NoMachine on the remote computer. Open the menu, Firefox and look for the phrase “NX Nomachine” in the open lookup box. Then go to the site of Nomachine in the section Downloads → NoMachine for Linux (Download) → NoMachine for Linux DEB amd64 → Download. Download and install:

  • And the finisher. It’s necessary to refresh the system. Menu → Terminal. In the open window of the terminal, insert:
sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get dist-upgrade

and push Enter. Then insert the password from the account and push Enter (the process of inserting the password isn’t displayed in the terminal, that’s ok).
After updating the list of packages, you’ll be asked to confirm your actions. Enter Y and push Enter. Great, when the process of updating is finished, close the terminal and restart the OS. Close the window of the VNC Console, you won’t need it anymore.

 

THE PROCESS OF INSTALLATION AND ADJUSTMENT OF VPRD IS OVER AT THAT.

Download the remote access client for your OS from the site of NoMachine (windows, mac, linux, ios or android). All you need to enter the remote computer is its IP address and the login/password from the account. The IP address of VPRD can be seen in the hosting control panel, in the section My Servers

All Done!

In this example, we have considered how to create your own Virtual Private Remote Desktop easily and fast. Operate your remote computer from anywhere, any device. That’s really very convenient – the computer which is always with you. And the one you can trust – for that’s Open Sorce, Linux. That’s VPRD.
